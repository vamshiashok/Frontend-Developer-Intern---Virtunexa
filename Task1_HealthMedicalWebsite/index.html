Health Tips

  • 1. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to maintain hydration.
  • 2. Eat a Balanced Diet: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your meals.
  • 3. Exercise Regularly: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity each week.
  • 4. Get Enough Sleep: Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support overall health.
  • 5. Manage Stress: Practice relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

Medical Services

  • 1. General Consultations: Comprehensive health check-ups and consultations with healthcare professionals.
  • 2. Diagnostic Services: Laboratory tests, imaging services (X-rays, MRIs), and other diagnostic procedures.
  • 3. Preventive Care: Vaccinations, screenings, and wellness exams to prevent illnesses.
  • 4. Specialty Care: Access to specialists for specific health issues, such as cardiology, dermatology, and orthopedics.
  • 5. Emergency Services: Immediate medical attention for urgent health concerns and emergencies.
